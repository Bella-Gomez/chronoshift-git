    About Us

    Company Origins

    The company, Chronshift Expeditions, was created based on the short story, “A Sound of Thunder” by Ray Bardbury. Using his story as a basis, Chronshift has recreated its time machine and services for potential customers even down to its policies and prices.

    Time traveling Technology, and other equipment

    Chronoshift Expeditions use their own time travel machine created off of the details given by Ray Bradbury. Before expeditions hunters will be equipped with special gear and weapons. While on expeditions hunters asked to only walk on the special antigravity path, this path was created to keep hunters from touching the world of the past.

    Safari Trips

    The company services hunting safaris to bag the hit in history. The company service is used to hunt and kill some of the biggest creatures from the past. From the Jurassic to the industrial revolution, Chronoshift is able to take customers to any time period they want. As the company logan states, “ Safaris to any year in the past. You name the animal. We take you there. you shoot it.”
