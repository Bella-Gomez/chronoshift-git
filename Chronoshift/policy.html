    Policies

    Chronshift Expeditions is not Responsible for Hunters

    With a payment, along with a signed release, future customers can pick any hit from the past. However, Chronshift Expedition is not responsible for any customer, only for the kill. Thus to avoid accidents, Chronshift only wants hunters who will not panic at first shot.

    Hunters must always listen to their safari guide

    Once the time traveller is on their safari, they are to listen completely to their guide while on the expedition. Safari guides will tell hunters when and where to shoot. If they say no shooting, then there should be no shooting. If hunters disobey, there will be a strict penalty of another ten thousand dollars and possible government involvement.

    Hunters must never step off the path

    While on expeditions, safari groups will be walked along a hovering metal path. Hunters are not allowed to step, at all, off the path. The Past Times are fragile, and anything can change the future, so Hunters are asked to tread carefully to avoid possible changes.
